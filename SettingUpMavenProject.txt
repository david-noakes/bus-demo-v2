To generate pom - simple, use

mvn archetype:generate  -DarchetypeGroupId=org.apache.maven.archetypes -DgroupId=com.dialog.gtfs_realtime -DartifactId=Bus-gtfs-realtime-visualiser

after this, add in the dependencies, and the build section from the onebusaway pom file

note that mvn architype:generate also generates a file structure:

Bus-gtfs-realtime-visualiser
  :src
	|:main
	|    :java
    |        :com
	|		     :dialog
    |                   :gtfs_realtime
    |                       :>App.java					   
	|:test					   
	     :java
		     :com
			     :dialog
                       :gtfs_realtime
							:>AppTest.java   (a JUnit)
